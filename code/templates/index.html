Triage Protocols

Visual verification rules and dispatch routing priorities

  • Priority 1
    Critical (Multi-Victim) Life Threat (2+ victims) + Fire. Requires SAR & suppression teams.
  • Priority 2
    Critical (Single-Victim) Life Threat (1 victim) + Fire. Immediate rescue & suppression.
  • Priority 3
    Lower A (Multi-Victim) Trapped Victims (2+), No Visible Fire. Search & rescue team.
  • Priority 4
    Lower A (Single-Victim) Trapped Victim (1), No Visible Fire. Search & rescue priority.
  • Priority 5
    Lower B (Active Fire) Active Fire, No Victims Spotted. Suppression priority.
  • Priority 6
    Normal Monitoring No active fire or victims detected. Standard visual monitoring.
